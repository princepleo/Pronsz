Nutrition Month

"Eat Right, Live Bright!"

Nutrition Month reminds everyone to eat healthy foods and live a healthy lifestyle. Eating nutritious meals gives us energy, helps us grow, and keeps our bodies strong.


Healthy foods include fruits, vegetables, fish, eggs, milk, rice, and whole grains. Drinking enough water and exercising every day are also important for good health.


Healthy Habits

✔ Eat a balanced diet.
✔ Drink 8 glasses of water every day.
✔ Exercise regularly.
✔ Get enough sleep.
✔ Avoid eating too much junk food.
